UCI Numerical Methods of Heat, Mass and Momentum Tansport: Laminar Flows - Winter 2014
Used the SIMPLE algorithm to solve a steady viscous laminar flow through a sudden expansion in an axisymmetric pipe. Analyzed the recirculation zone by plotting the streamlines, vorticity, shear stress, velocity and pressure fields. Compared results with previous publications to validate the solution. Programmed the SIMPLER algorithm and compared the computation time with that of SIMPLE.

UCI Bio-Robotics - Fall 2014
Surveyed state of the art approaches for robotic and mechatronic devices that support and enhance human movement for rehabilitation and surgery. Designed decoder in Mat-Lab for a brain computer interface for a locked in patient. Decoder took input from roughly 1000 neurons and moved a cursor in a direction dependent on the firing rates of those brain neurons.

UCI Computational Fluid Dynamics - Fall 2014
Programmed common numerical methods for solving compressible Euler and Navier-Stokes equations at subsonic, transonic, and supersonic speeds. Used the numeric schemes to simulate a shock tube, and a supersonic nozzle flow with shock capture.
